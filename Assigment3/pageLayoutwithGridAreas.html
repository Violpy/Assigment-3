Theatre

Theatre is the art of performing live stories on stage with actors, sets, lighting, and sometimes music. It has different types like tragedy, comedy, drama, and experimental theatre.

Examples

  • Hamlet – a tragedy by Shakespeare
  • The Importance of Being Earnest – a comedy by Oscar Wilde
  • A Streetcar Named Desire – modern drama
  • Waiting for Godot – experimental theatre

Musicals

Musicals are like theatre but with singing and dancing woven into the story.

Examples

  • Phantom of the Opera
  • Heathers
  • West Side Story
  • Hamilton
  • Wicked
  • Dear Evan Hansen

Difference

Theatre = acting, dialogue, monologues.
Musicals = all of that PLUS songs & choreography.
